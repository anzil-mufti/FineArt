OUR WORKS

Services Offered

Wedding Photography and Videography

We shoot the feeling of each particular wedding day, We don’t shoot what it looks like, we shoot what it feels like so that our photographs and videos will still hold their power in coming years. We believe weddings should be meaningful and memorable. What we care most about is connecting with the person on the other side of the lens to bring out genuine warm emotions throughout the event. This translates into our ability to incorporate live ceremonies like dwarchar, jaimal, fere, vidai, nikah etc.. into a timeless story that emphasizes the personality of each client.

Event Shooting

We shoot the live events, shooting great wedding videos. We shoot the stunning videos for your special day without missing any precious moment of your big day. The shooting of the wedding is performed as per subtle scripts that have been pre planned.

Outdoor Photography

When it comes to outdoor photography, we've a great experience of capturing the moments and portraying them in a beautiful manner. We create an expressive diorama from your outdoor adventures, creating the scenes where the composition becomes more personal to the viewer. We consider how best we can encapsulate the scenery, this includes anything from lens choice to shooting techniques that will enhance the photographs. We try to put ourselves at that time and place that allows the best opportunity for good light, frames, angles, clouds, etc.

ABOUT US

FineArt Studio

We are the one of leading wedding and film making company in the state of Uttar Pradesh, based in heart of Kanpur, at Hospital Road, Parade established 87 years ago.

It all started in the summer of “1934” when a 22 years young slender boy(our grand-father) from the narrow lanes of old Kanpur against all odds decided to pursue his passion and make it his livelihood, since then FineArt studio has come a long way, now it is the descendants scaling new heights, a perfect blend of youth and experience.

Our grand-father started with a pre-owned Kodak Retina lens which served our livelihood. From years of savings, he bought a new lens of Argus which led him to success at that era and from then as the technology improved, he along with his sons upgraded the cameras and his lenses accordingly.

We are the team of creative wedding photographers with a passion for candid wedding photography making us more popular as one of the best wedding photographers in Kanpur. We are specialised to capture artistically different unique moments that the couple wish to be present in the memories.

Gathering moments for the future: The photographs from your wedding will provide memories that last a lifetime. They’re the only mementos that can invoke the myriad of emotions felt on your wedding day years after the event. Taking extra care in selecting the right wedding photographer and planning ahead for the shots that you want will go a long way in securing your photo album’s status as an attraction centerpiece rather than a cobweb collector in some dusty attic.

Specialties Include: We become fully integrated within the event to capture a moment, and we build relationships with the people around us and gain their trust, so that they feel relaxed and free enough to express themselves naturally. We love to represent both the formality of the occasion and those off-guard moments which truly reflect the spirit of the event.

Signature Aesthetic: A contemporary, off-the-cuff approach that utilizes an event's moments against our experience with composition, angles, and light. Then we focus on the unexpected, finding authentic moments and new perspectives which allow us to elevate our work with each and every couple. We want images to have balance and feel very real with a hint of curated artistry.
